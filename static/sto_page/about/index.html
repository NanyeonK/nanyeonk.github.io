About

Real Estate STO Review is a weekly publication examining the intersection of real estate finance and security token offerings. The publication serves both practitioners working in tokenized real estate and researchers studying the evolving landscape of digital securities.

Each week we cover recent developments in the STO market, analyze regulatory changes, and discuss open research questions. Our wiki section provides a growing knowledge base of core concepts for readers at any level of familiarity with the topic.

This publication is maintained by Yeonchan Kang, a researcher in real estate finance and asset pricing.
