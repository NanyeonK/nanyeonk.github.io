I am a PhD student in Economics at Sungkyunkwan University. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning and financial economics, with a focus on real estate markets, asset pricing, and forecasting. My current work examines how interpretable machine-learning methods can improve housing price prediction, the role of evening economic activity in commercial real estate valuation, and predictive modeling in commodity and digital asset markets. I am particularly interested in bridging methodological advances in machine learning with substantive economic questions—ensuring that predictive models are not only accurate but also economically interpretable.
